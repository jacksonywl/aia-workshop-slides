Framework

The Lead Equation

LEADS = REACH √ó RELEVANCE

That's it. Two ingredients.

In Plain English

REACH

"How many people see me?"

  • Posting content gets reach
  • Outreach gets reach
  • Commenting gets reach

RELEVANCE

"Does what I say matter to them?"

  • Generic = low relevance
  • Specific to their problem = high
  • Personalized = very high

You need BOTH. One without the other = no leads.

The Nasi Lemak Stall
üçö

REACH

The stall is on a busy corner with a big sign (people walk by and see it)

RELEVANCE

The sign says "Hungry? Best nasi lemak in PJ ‚Äî ready in 5 minutes" not just "Food sold here"

Busy corner + the right message = the stall sells out by 10am

Important Insight

Trust is NOT a lead variable

You don't need someone to trust you for them to raise their hand.

They just need to see something relevant to their problem.

Trust comes later ‚Äî when they decide whether to PAY you. That's Module 8 and 9.

How the 4 Doors Work

1
Warm Outreach
People who already know you exist ‚Äî friends, connections, groups you're active in. The FASTEST path to your first client.
2
Cold Outreach
Strangers who don't know you ‚Äî cold DMs, emails, job applications. Wider reach, more volume, requires personalization.
3
Free Content
Posting content that attracts people to you. Slow at first, but compounds over time. Leads come to YOU.
4
Paid Ads (Later)
Facebook, Google, TikTok ads. Requires budget ‚Äî save this for when you're earning and ready to scale.

Which Door First?

"I need money THIS WEEK" Door 1: Warm
"I need money THIS MONTH" Door 2: Cold
"I want leads EVERY month" Door 3: Content
"I want a remote job" Door 2: Applications

Best strategy: Open Doors 1-3 simultaneously. Door 4 when you have budget.

Examples by Industry
Fitness Coaches
Door 1: Message coaches you follow with a free audit offer
Door 2: DM coaches with 5K-50K followers who lack landing pages
Door 3: Post "3 things every coach's landing page needs"
Dental Clinics
Door 1: Visit local clinics, leave your card
Door 2: Email clinics with fewer than 50 Google reviews
Door 3: Post "I audited 10 dental clinics' Google profiles"
Business Coaches
Door 1: Message coaches in your network about funnels
Door 2: DM coaches with basic Carrd/Linktree pages
Door 3: Post "This coach's bookings went from 2 to 8/week"

Platform Follows Industry

Where are your clients?

  • Professional Services: LinkedIn
  • Local Businesses: Google Maps, phone calls, in-person
  • Agencies: LinkedIn, direct email

Match your outreach method to how the client actually operates.

The 30-Day Lead Sprint

Week Door 1 (Warm) Door 2 (Cold) Door 3 (Content)
Week 1 10 warm contacts 10 cold messages 3 posts
Week 2 Follow up 15 cold messages 3 posts
Week 3 10 more warm 15 + follow up 5 posts
Week 4 Follow up all 20 + follow up 5 posts

The Math

Total in 30 days:

  • 20 warm contacts
  • 60 cold outreach messages
  • 16 content pieces

Expected conversations: 6-13

If you close 1-2? That's $500-$2,000.

You don't need 100 clients. You need 1-2 clients.
