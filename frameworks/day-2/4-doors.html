Door 1: Warm Outreach

Reaching out to people who already know you exist

  • Friends and connections
  • Facebook groups you're active in
  • People who've interacted with your content
  • Anyone who knows your name
Speed: Days Difficulty: Easy

This is where your first client is hiding.

Door 2: Cold Outreach

Reaching out to strangers who don't know you

  • Cold DMs and cold emails
  • Job applications
  • Businesses found on Google Maps
  • LinkedIn outreach to decision-makers
Speed: Weeks Difficulty: Medium

Wider reach. More volume. Personalization is key.

Door 3: Free Content

Posting content that attracts people to you

  • Value posts that teach something
  • Proof posts showing results
  • Behind-the-scenes content
  • Bold opinions that differentiate you
Speed: Weeks-Months Difficulty: Hard at first, easiest long-term

Compounds over time. Leads come to YOU.
