Section C

The Client's POV

Think like a business owner, not a marketer.

When Coach Danny hires you...

He's NOT thinking: "I need someone who's good at Canva."

"I'm stuck at 10 clients a month. I need to get to 30. My Instagram gets views but nobody signs up for the challenge. Something's broken between 'they watch my video' and 'they join the challenge.' Can you fix THAT?"

The 3 Questions Every Client Asks

(Even if they don't say it out loud)

1
"Do you understand MY business?"
What they say: "So what do you know about [my industry]?"
2
"Can you actually help me get results?"
What they say: "What have you done before?"
3
"Is this going to be worth the money?"
What they say: "How much do you charge?"
