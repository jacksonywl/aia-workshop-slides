Door 1

Warm Outreach Formula

The Warm Message Template
Hey [Name],

I've been learning AI marketing and noticed [specific observation about their business].

I'd love to [specific offer -- audit, mock-up, idea] for you, totally free. Just want to practice and help out.

Would you be open to that?

Key: Genuine, specific, no pressure.

Warm Outreach Example
Scenario: A fitness coach you follow in a Facebook group
Hey Coach Sarah!

I've been in this group for a while and I love your transformation posts. I noticed your link-in-bio goes to a basic Linktree -- I've been learning AI marketing and I think I could build you a proper landing page that converts visitors into clients.

Would you be open to me creating a free mock-up? No strings attached -- I'm building my portfolio and your brand would be a great project.

Either way, keep crushing it!

Personal. Specific. Offering value first.

Door 2

Cold Outreach Formula

The Cold Message Template
Hi [Name / Business],

I found your [website / Google listing / social media] and noticed [1 specific problem you spotted].

I specialize in [what you do] and I put together a quick [audit / suggestion / mock-up] for you -- [attach or describe it].

Worth a quick look?

Key: Lead with value. Never pitch first. Give something specific.

Cold Outreach Example 1
Scenario: A dental clinic with 18 Google reviews (competitors have 200+)
Hi Dr. Tan,

I came across Smile Dental Clinic on Google Maps. You have great ratings (4.8 stars!) but I noticed you only have 18 reviews -- most top-ranking clinics in PJ have 150+.

I put together a quick 3-step plan that could help you get to 50+ reviews in 30 days using a simple follow-up system. Happy to share it if you're interested.

No cost, no catch -- just thought it might help.

Specific problem + specific solution = reply.

Cold Outreach Example 2
Scenario: Applying for a marketing role at a startup
Hi Amir,

I saw the Marketing Executive role at GrowthLab. Before applying, I took a look at your landing page and put together 3 quick suggestions that could improve your conversion rate.

I also mocked up a redesigned hero section using AI tools -- took about 20 minutes.

Here's the link: [portfolio link]

Would love to chat if the role is still open.

Job seekers: Show the work BEFORE the interview. Stand out instantly.

Door 3

Content as Lead Gen

Content Type Lead Gen Angle CTA
Value Post Teach something specific to your niche "DM me [KEYWORD] for the full checklist"
Proof Post Show a result you created "Want me to do this for your business? DM me"
Audit Post Publicly audit a business (with permission) "I'll do this for 3 businesses free -- comment below"
POV Post Strong opinion that attracts ideal clients "Agree? Follow for more marketing breakdowns"

Every post should have a clear CTA. No CTA = wasted reach.

Lead Gen Post Example

Audit Post -- Dental Clinic

I audited 5 dental clinics in PJ. Here's what I found:

- 4 out of 5 had fewer than 50 Google reviews
- 3 had no booking link on their Google listing
- 2 had websites that take 8+ seconds to load
- 0 had a follow-up system for new patients

These are easy fixes that could bring in 10-20 new patients/month.

I'll audit 3 more clinics for free this week. Drop a comment or DM me "AUDIT" if you want yours done.

Framework

The E.V.I. Comment

E
ENGAGE
Acknowledge their post. Show you actually read it. Be genuine.
V
VALUE
Add something useful -- a tip, perspective, or relevant experience.
I
INVITE
Open the door for further conversation -- a question, a suggestion to connect.

One good comment > 10 generic ones.

Comparison
Generic Comment
"Great post! Very insightful. Thanks for sharing!"

Zero value. Looks like a bot. Nobody clicks your profile.

E.V.I. Comment
"This is spot on -- I just ran an audit on 5 dental clinics and found the exact same problem with Google reviews. One thing I'd add: a simple QR code at the reception desk can 3x review collection. Would love to compare notes!"

Engaged. Added value. Invited conversation. Profile clicks guaranteed.

Where to Comment + Daily Routine

WHERE

  • Facebook Groups in your niche
  • LinkedIn posts by industry leaders
  • Instagram posts by target clients
  • Competitor comment sections

DAILY: 10 MINUTES

  • Find 5 posts in your niche
  • Leave 5 E.V.I. comments
  • Reply to anyone who responds
  • Check new DMs from profile visits

10 minutes a day. 5 comments. Compounding visibility.

Framework

The Audit Gift

1
PICK
Choose a real business. Someone you want to work with.
2
AUDIT
Review their online presence. Find 3-5 specific problems.
3
GIFT
Package the findings + solutions. Send it to them for free.

The Audit Gift is the single most effective outreach tool you have.

What to Look For

Website
Slow loading? No mobile version?
Unclear headline? No CTA?
Broken links? Outdated design?
Google Business
Few reviews? No photos?
Wrong hours? Missing categories?
No booking link? Bad description?
Social Media
No bio? No link in bio?
Inconsistent posting? No CTA?
Low engagement? No replies?
Landing Page
No landing page at all?
Weak headline? No proof?
Multiple CTAs? No urgency?

You don't need to be an expert. You need fresh eyes.

Audit Example

Dr. Lim's Dental Clinic

FINDINGS:

  • Google listing: 23 reviews (competitors avg 180+)
  • Website loads in 9 seconds (should be under 3)
  • No online booking -- only a phone number
  • Facebook page hasn't posted in 4 months
  • No landing page for specific services (whitening, braces)

RECOMMENDATIONS:

  • Set up a review collection system (QR code at reception)
  • Add online booking widget to website + Google listing
  • Create 1 landing page for teeth whitening service
