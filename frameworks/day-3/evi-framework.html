Day 3 Framework

The E.V.I. Comment Strategy

Turn every comment into a client magnet. 3 parts. 30 seconds. Massive results.

Why Commenting Works

👁
Free Visibility
Your comment appears under posts with thousands of views. Free exposure to their entire audience.
🤝
Builds Relationships
Creators notice who comments consistently. You become familiar. Familiar leads to trust.
🎯
Attracts Clients
A smart comment makes people click your profile. Your profile is your storefront. They walk in.

But only if you comment the right way.

The Framework
E
Engage
Acknowledge what they said
V
Value
Add something useful
I
Invite
Subtle reason to check you out

Every comment, every time. 3 sentences. 30 seconds.

E
Engage
Acknowledge what they said

Show the person you actually read their content. Reference something specific. Don't just say "great post" -- prove you paid attention.

Example
"Your point about pricing based on value instead of hours really hit home..."

This separates you from the 99% of generic comments.

V
Value
Add something useful

Add a tip, insight, or personal experience that builds on their point. Don't just agree -- extend the conversation with something others can learn from.

Example
"I started doing this with my freelance clients last month and my close rate went from 20% to 45%. The key was framing the price around ROI, not deliverables."

This is what makes people think: "Who is this person? Let me check their profile."

I
Invite
Subtle reason to check you out

Give a soft invitation -- not a sales pitch. Mention something relevant on your page, a resource you made, or just leave them curious enough to click.

Example
"I actually wrote a breakdown of 3 pricing frameworks that work for freelancers -- it's on my page if you're interested."

Never "hire me." Always "I have something useful."
