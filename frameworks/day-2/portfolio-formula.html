The Portfolio Formula

A portfolio doesn't need to be fancy. It needs to be CLEAR. 5 sections. Done in 30 minutes.

1 HEADLINE
"I'm [Name], an AI Marketer who helps [X] with [Y]"
2 WHAT I DO
3-4 bullet points of services
3 MY WORK
Content pieces from Module 5 + mock-ups
4 ABOUT ME
Short paragraph + photo
5 CONTACT
"DM me on Facebook" or email or booking link
1 HEADLINE

Use your Mirror Test statement from Module 3. This goes at the very top.

"I'm Sarah, an AI Marketer who helps fitness coaches get more paying clients through landing pages and content."

First thing they see. Make it crystal clear.

2 WHAT I DO

List your services in 3-4 bullet points. Keep it simple.

  • Landing pages that convert visitors into leads
  • Social media content that builds authority
  • Email sequences that nurture leads automatically
  • Brand visuals that look professional

Use your 4 Core Skills from Module 3.

3 MY WORK

Show proof. This is where your content pieces from Module 5 go.

  • 3 content pieces you created today
  • Landing page mock-ups
  • Design samples
  • Screenshots of results (when you have them)

This is your proof that you know what you're doing.

Sections 4 & 5

4 ABOUT ME

3-4 sentences. Who you are, why you do this, what you believe. Add a photo to make it personal.

5 CONTACT

ONE clear way to reach you. DM link, email, or booking page. Make the next step obvious.
