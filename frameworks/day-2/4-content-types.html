VALUE Content

Teach something useful. Gets shared, builds authority.

Example formats:

  • "3 things every [industry] needs for [result]"
  • "The mistake 90% of [audience] make"
  • "How to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]"
  • "I audited 10 [businesses]. Here's what I found."

40% of your content mix

PROOF Content

Show results. Gets clients, builds trust.

Example formats:

  • "Before: [problem]. After: [result]. Here's what I did."
  • "I helped [client type] achieve [specific outcome]"
  • Testimonial + screenshot
  • Case study with numbers

25% of your content mix

BEHIND-THE-SCENES Content

Show the process. Gets engagement, builds relatability.

Example formats:

  • "Watch me build [X] in [time] using AI"
  • "Here's my workflow for creating [deliverable]"
  • "A day in my life as an AI Marketer"
  • "The tools I use every day"

20% of your content mix

POV Content

Share an opinion. Gets attention, builds differentiation.

Example formats:

  • "Unpopular opinion: [bold statement]"
  • "Why most [audience] fail at [thing]"
  • "Hot take: [industry insight]"
  • "The truth about [common belief]"

15% of your content mix
