In Plain English

"How many people see me?"

You need BOTH. One without the other = no leads.

REACH
"How many of the right people see my message?"
  • Posting content gets reach
  • Outreach gets reach
  • Commenting gets reach
  • Ads get reach (paid)
RELEVANCE
"How much does my message match their actual problem?"
  • Generic: "I do marketing" = low
  • Specific: "I help dentists get more patients" = high
  • Personalized outreach = very high
  • Content that solves their problem = high

The Two Power Moves

CONTENT
Reach at scale

One post can be seen by thousands. Content compounds over time. This is Day 2's focus.

OUTREACH
Relevance at depth

Personalized messages have higher conversion. Direct conversations build relationships. This is Day 3's focus.

Content gives you reach. Outreach gives you relevance.
Use both.
