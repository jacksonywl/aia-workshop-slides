Sections 1 & 2

1 HEADLINE
  • Clear, specific promise
  • Use numbers if possible
  • Focus on the OUTCOME
  • Should pass the "so what?" test
2 PROBLEM
  • Name their pain
  • Show you understand
  • Be specific, not generic
  • "If this resonates, keep reading"

Sections 3, 4, 5

3
SOLUTION
What you offer. How it works. Keep it simple ‚Äî 2-3 sentences or bullets.
4
PROOF
Numbers, testimonials, awards, before/after. Whatever builds credibility.
5
CTA (Call to Action)
ONE button. ONE action. "Book Now" or "Get Started" or "DM Me". No multiple options.
