Step 1: BRIEF

Write a 2-3 sentence description of what you want

Example brief:

"I want to write a Value post about 3 things every fitness coach's landing page needs. It should be practical and end with a CTA to DM me."

Tip: Just tell the AI what you'd say to a friend. That's your brief.

Step 2: AI DRAFT

Feed the brief to AI. Get a full draft back.

AI generates:

  • Full draft based on your brief
  • Structure and flow
  • Headlines and hooks
  • Call-to-action options

AI gives you 80% of the work. Now make it yours.

Step 3: HUMAN EDIT

Read the draft. Cut the fluff. Make it sound natural.

AI Problem How to Fix
Too formal Shorten sentences. Use "you" and "I".
Too generic Add a specific detail or number
No opinion Add your take: "Honestly? I'd start with..."
Too long Cut by 30%. Ruthlessly.
The Edit in Action
AI Says (Robot)
"It is essential to optimize your landing page to maximize conversion rates and ensure potential clients understand the value proposition."
You Say (Human)
"Here's the thing — most coaches skip this. And it's costing them 10+ clients a month."

Same idea. Different voice. One sounds like a robot. One sounds like you.

Step 4: DESIGN

Create the visual. Make it look good.

  • Social media graphic — headline + visual
  • Carousel — multiple slides with key points
  • Video thumbnail — eye-catching preview
  • Simple text post — no graphic needed

Use Canva, AI design tools, or templates. Don't overthink it.

The Key Insight

"I can't write" is dead

You don't need to write. You need to DIRECT.

You didn't stare at a blank page. You told the AI what you wanted, it gave you 80% of the work, and you made it yours.
