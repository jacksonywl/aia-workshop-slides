The Value Equation

The formula that makes any offer irresistible. Understand this and you'll know exactly how to position what you sell.

The Formula
VALUE
=
Dream Outcome
×
Likelihood
Time Delay
×
Effort

What each part means

⬆️ Maximize (Top)
Dream Outcome

How BIG is the result they want?

Perceived Likelihood

How likely do they BELIEVE it'll work?

⬇️ Minimize (Bottom)
Time Delay

How LONG until they see results?

Effort & Sacrifice

How much WORK do they have to do?

In plain English

People pay more when:

  • The dream outcome is BIGGER
  • They believe it's MORE LIKELY to work
  • They get results FASTER
  • They have to do LESS WORK

How it changes your offer

❌ Before (Weak)

"I'll make you a landing page"

✅ After (Value Equation)

"I'll build you a landing page that converts visitors into leads — delivered in 5 days, and you don't have to write a single word"

Dream: Convert visitors Likelihood: Implied expertise Time: 5 days Effort: Zero writing

Your Turn

Apply this to your offer

Ask yourself:

  • What's the dream outcome for my client?
  • What proof do I have that it works?
  • How fast can I deliver?
  • How easy is it for them?

The more you can boost the top and reduce the bottom, the more valuable your offer becomes.

Value is a formula

VALUE
=
Dream × Likelihood
Time × Effort

Use this formula every time you craft an offer. It's the difference between "meh" and "take my money."
