Module 8: The Conversation

How do I handle a client call or job interview with confidence?

The conversation isn't about convincing.

It's about understanding.

The best salespeople don't talk the most.
They listen the most.

Why People Freeze

They think it's about SELLING.

Selling feels pushy. Selling feels sleazy. Selling feels fake.

The truth:

It's not about selling. It's about finding out if you can HELP.

Framework

The 5-Step Conversation

1
OPEN
2-3 min
2
DISCOVER
10-15 min
3
BRIDGE
3-5 min
4
OFFER
5-10 min
5
HANDLE
5-10 min

Works for client calls. Works for job interviews. Same structure.

Step 1

OPEN -- Build Rapport

Be human. Not a robot.

  • Thank them for their time
  • Make a genuine observation (about their business, content, etc.)
  • Set the agenda: "Here's what I was thinking for this call..."
  • Ask a simple question to get them talking

2-3 minutes. That's it. Then move to DISCOVER.

Step 2

DISCOVER -- Ask & Listen

This is the MOST important step.

Ask questions. LISTEN to the answers. Take notes. Don't pitch yet.

Whoever talks most LOSES.

Your goal: understand their problem, their situation, and their desired outcome.

Discovery Questions: Clients

1
"Tell me about your business -- what do you do?"
2
"How are you currently getting customers?"
3
"What's working? What's not?"
4
"If we could fix ONE thing, what would make the biggest difference?"
5
"Have you tried anything before? What happened?"
6
"What would success look like for you in the next 90 days?"
7
"Is there a budget or timeline you're working with?"

Discovery Questions: Interviews

1
"What does success look like for someone in this role in the first 90 days?"
2
"What's the biggest marketing challenge the team is facing right now?"
3
"What tools and platforms does the team currently use?"
4
"Who would I be working with most closely?"
5
"What made you decide to hire for this role now?"

These questions show you're serious, prepared, and thinking like an owner.

Step 3

BRIDGE -- Connect Their Problem to Your Solution

The magic phrase:

"Based on what you told me, here's what I think would help..."

Summarize what they said. Show you listened. Then transition to what you can do about it.

3-5 minutes. This is the turning point of the conversation.

Step 4

OFFER -- Present Your Solution

Be specific. Not vague.

VAGUE

"I can help with your marketing"

SPECIFIC

"I'll build you a landing page for your whitening service, set up a Google review system, and create 4 social posts -- all in 2 weeks."

Specific offer + specific timeline = trust.

Step 5

HANDLE -- Answer Honestly

They WILL have concerns. That's normal.

  • "How much experience do you have?"
    Be honest. Show your portfolio. Let the work speak.
  • "What if it doesn't work?"
    Offer a small starter project first. Reduce their risk.
  • "Can I think about it?"
    Of course. Set a follow-up date. Don't pressure.

Trust beats bluffing. Every time.

The Doctor Analogy
🩺
Doctor
1. "What's wrong?" (symptoms)
2. "How long?" (history)
3. "Here's what I think" (diagnosis)
4. "Here's the plan" (treatment)
5. "Any questions?" (handle)
📈
AI Marketer
1. "Tell me about your business" (symptoms)
2. "What have you tried?" (history)
3. "Based on what you said..." (diagnosis)
4. "Here's what I'd do" (treatment)
5. "What questions do you have?" (handle)

A doctor never walks in and says "You need surgery!" before asking questions.
Neither should you.

Example Walkthrough
Client: Coach Danny -- fitness coach, 8K followers, no landing page, getting 2 clients/month
1. OPEN
"Thanks for jumping on, Danny. I loved that transformation post you did last week -- really powerful stuff."
2. DISCOVER
"So tell me -- how are you currently getting clients? ... What's been your biggest frustration?"
3. BRIDGE
"So it sounds like you're getting attention but losing people because there's no clear next step after they find you..."
4. OFFER
"Here's what I'd do: build you a landing page for your 12-week program, set up an email sequence for leads, and create 4 posts. Two weeks, done."
5. HANDLE
"Want to start with just the landing page? If you like it, we do the rest."

The Job Interview Flip

Same 5 steps. Reframed for interviews.

Step Client Call Job Interview
OPEN Build rapport Thank them, show enthusiasm
DISCOVER Ask about their problem Ask about the role's challenges
BRIDGE "Based on what you said..." "Based on what you need..."
OFFER Present your solution Show how you'd solve it (with examples)
HANDLE Answer objections Address experience gaps honestly
Job Track Secret Weapon

The 30/60/90 Day Plan

Day 1-30
Learn & Audit
Understand the business
Audit current marketing
Identify quick wins
Deliver 2-3 small wins
Day 31-60
Build & Launch
Implement top priorities
Set up systems
Start content calendar
Show first results
Day 61-90
Scale & Optimize
Double down on what works
Cut what doesn't
Present results report
Propose next quarter plan

Bring this to the interview. 99% of candidates won't.

After Each Round, Ask

🤔
"Did I ask enough questions before offering?"
💬
"Was my offer specific or vague?"
💪
"Did I handle objections honestly or did I bluff?"
🤝
"Would you actually hire me / buy from me?"
💡
"What's ONE thing I should improve?"

Honest feedback now saves embarrassing mistakes later.
