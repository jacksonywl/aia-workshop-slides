Step 1: BRIEF

Write a 2-3 sentence description of what you want

Example:

"I want to write a Value post about 3 things every fitness coach's landing page needs. It should be practical and end with a CTA to DM me."

Tip: Just tell AI what you'd say to a friend.

Step 2: AI DRAFT

Feed the brief to AI. Get a full draft back.

AI gives you:

  • Full draft based on your brief
  • Structure and flow
  • Headlines and hooks
  • Call-to-action options

AI gives you 80% of the work. Now make it yours.

Step 3: HUMAN EDIT

Read the draft. Cut the fluff. Make it sound natural.

AI: Too formal
Shorten sentences. Use "you" and "I".
AI: Too generic
Add a specific detail or number.
AI: No opinion
Add your take: "Honestly? I'd start with..."
AI: Too long
Cut by 30%. Ruthlessly.
The Edit in Action
AI Says (Robot)
"It is essential to optimize your landing page to maximize conversion rates and ensure potential clients understand the value proposition."
You Say (Human)
"Here's the thing ‚Äî most coaches skip this. And it's costing them 10+ clients a month."

Same idea. Different voice. One sounds like a robot. One sounds like you.

Step 4: DESIGN

Create the visual. Make it look good.

  • Social media graphic ‚Äî headline + visual
  • Carousel ‚Äî multiple slides with key points
  • Video thumbnail ‚Äî eye-catching preview
  • Simple text post ‚Äî no graphic needed

Use Canva or AI tools. Don't overthink it.
