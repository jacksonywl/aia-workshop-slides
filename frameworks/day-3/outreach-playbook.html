Simple. Repeatable. Works every time.

Step 1: WHO

Define exactly who you're reaching out to

  • 1 Your ideal client profile -- who has the problem you solve?
  • 2 Their role or business type -- coach, restaurant owner, agency?
  • 3 Their current pain -- what keeps them up at night?
  • 4 Their budget level -- can they afford to pay you?

If you can't describe them in one sentence, you're not specific enough.

Step 2: WHERE

Find where they hang out online

Instagram
Follow hashtags, explore pages, competitor followers
Facebook Groups
Join groups where your clients ask questions
LinkedIn
Search by job title, industry, location
Google Maps
Local businesses with bad websites or no social
Twitter / X
Follow industry conversations, reply to threads
TikTok
Comment on creators in your niche

Go where they already are. Don't make them come to you.

Step 3: WHAT

The message -- value first, not a pitch

Lead with value. Never with a pitch.
  • X "Hey, I do social media management. Want to work together?"
  • ✓ "Hey, I noticed your Reels are getting good views but your bio doesn't convert. Quick tip: add a clear CTA."

Give something useful before you ask for anything.

Warm Outreach

The Warm Message Template

Warm Template

"Hey [Name]! I saw your [post/story/update] about [topic] -- really cool.

I actually just helped someone with [similar problem] and got them [specific result].

I had a quick idea for you -- want me to share it?"

✓ Personal -- references something specific
✓ Proof -- shows you've done this before
✓ Permission-based -- asks if they want more
Cold Outreach

The Cold Message Template

Cold Template

"Hey [Name], I came across your [business/page/website] and noticed [specific observation].

I work with [similar businesses] to help them [specific outcome].

I put together a quick [idea/audit/suggestion] -- would it be okay to send it over?"

✓ Shows you've done research on THEM
✓ Leads with a specific observation, not a pitch
✓ Offers value upfront -- a free idea or audit

Step 4: FOLLOW

Most people quit after 1 message. Don't be most people.

Day 1 Send the first message (value-first)
Day 3-5 Follow up: "Hey, just checking -- did you see my note?"
Day 7-10 Add more value: share a resource, tip, or case study
Day 14 Final check-in: "No worries if the timing is off -- just wanted to help"

80% of sales happen after the 5th touchpoint. Most people give up after the 1st.
