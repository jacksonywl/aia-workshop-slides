Week 1
Days 1-7
Start the Machine
  • → Finalize your offer -- one specific service, one clear outcome
  • → Set up your storefront -- optimize your profile/landing page
  • → Make your hit list -- 50 people to reach out to (warm + cold)
  • → Send 10 outreach messages per day using the Outreach Playbook
  • → Post 3 pieces of content using the Content Machine
Week 1 target
50 messages sent + 3 posts published
Week 2
Days 8-14
Build Momentum
  • → Follow up on Week 1 messages -- use the FOLLOW step
  • → Send 10 more outreach messages per day -- new people
  • → Leave 10 E.V.I. comments per day on target accounts
  • → Post 3 more pieces of content -- aim for at least 1 proof post
  • → Book 2-3 discovery calls from interested replies
Week 2 target
100+ total messages + 2-3 calls booked
Week 3
Days 15-21
Close
  • → Run discovery calls using the 5-Step Conversation
  • → Send proposals within 24 hours of each call
  • → Follow up on proposals -- don't wait for them to come to you
  • → Close 2-3 starter projects at $300-$500 each
  • → Continue outreach -- don't stop the pipeline
Week 3 target
2-3 clients signed + invoices sent
Week 4
Days 22-30
Get Paid
  • → Deliver the work -- overdeliver on your first clients
  • → Collect payment -- send invoice immediately, follow up
  • → Ask for a testimonial -- screenshot the results + their words
  • → Upsell or get a referral -- "know anyone else who needs this?"
  • → Post proof content -- share the win (with permission)
Week 4 target
$1,000+ collected + 1-2 testimonials
